~ Welcome to the *Blink* Prototype ~

Controls

W (Hold): Slow Time

S (Hold): Stop Time

D (Hold): Rewind Time

A (Hold): Attack Time

Spacebar: Jump

Left/Right Arrows for Movement


Warning: It is dangerous to go alone, there are 'features' everywhere.
Ok so they are bugs. But listen. It's gonna be sweet. You'll use your time powers to make it through obstacles and bosses.
Protip: Hold a time power and act, while your foes watch in horror as you slip outside the fabric of time.
